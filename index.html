FAQ

Questions creators actually ask.

Will Instagram flag or down-rank AI content from VynixFlow?

No. Every export is cleaned of AI fingerprints, metadata signatures, and encoder flags. The files read like native phone photos and videos to platform algorithms.

What exactly can I generate? What can't I?

You can generate lifestyle, fashion, fitness, swimwear, lingerie, boudoir, cosplay — the full range most creators actually post. We don't generate explicit nudity or fully NSFW content, by design.

Is the model unique to me? Will other users get the same creator?

Every model is unique to your account. Locked seeds mean the same creator stays exclusively yours — nobody else can generate her.

Do you train on my uploads or outputs?

Never. Your reference photos, models, and generated content stay private to your account. No training, no sharing, no public gallery.

Can I import a creator or real person I already have photos of?

Yes — if it's you, or someone who has clearly consented. Upload a reference photo to lock the likeness. We require that you have full rights to any person you import.

Is this Stripe-safe? Will my payments get flagged?

Yes. Because we stay in suggestive-SFW territory and don't do explicit content, we use standard Stripe payment rails. No processor surprises.
